Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General Questions

  • What time does the Open Day start and end?

    The Open Day runs from 9:30 AM to 4:00 PM. Morning ticket holders can arrive between 9:30 AM – 1:00 PM, and afternoon ticket holders can arrive from 1:00 PM – 4:00 PM.

  • Where do I check in?

    Check in at the Main Campus Reception when you arrive. Make sure to have your QR code ready for scanning.

Booking and Tickets

  • Do I need to book talks and tours in advance?

    Yes, we encourage you to book your places for talks and tours in advance, as spaces may be limited. You can do this through the Talks and Tours Booking Page.

  • Is there a fee to attend the Open Day?

    No, attending the Open Day is free of charge. However, you need to register for a ticket to access the campus.

Travel and Accessibility

  • How do I get to St Mary’s University?

    St Mary’s is easily accessible by train, bus, car, or bike. For detailed travel information, visit the "How to Get to St Mary’s" section in our app.

  • Is parking available on campus?

    Limited parking is available on campus, so we recommend using public transport where possible.

  • What if I have special assistance requirements?

    If you require any special assistance, please contact us at opendays@stmarys.ac.uk ahead of your visit so we can accommodate your needs.

On the Day

  • What should I bring with me?

    Bring your QR code, a form of ID, and any questions you may have about studying at St Mary’s.

  • Will there be food and drink available?

    Yes, there will be various food and drink options available on campus throughout the day.

  • Can I bring family or friends?

    Absolutely! You are welcome to bring family and friends along to help you explore the campus and learn more about our programs.

Contact Us

  • Who can I contact if I have more questions?

    For any further inquiries, feel free to reach out to us at opendays@stmarys.ac.uk or visit our support desk on the day of the event.

We look forward to welcoming you to St Mary’s University and hope you have a fantastic Open Day experience!

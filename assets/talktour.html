Welcome to the Talks and Tours Booking Page!

At St Mary’s University, we offer various informative sessions and engaging campus tours during our Open Day. Here’s how you can reserve your spots:

Booking Information:

  • Explore the Schedule: Browse through the list of talks and tours available, including subject-specific sessions, accommodation talks, and insights into student life.
  • Select Your Sessions: Click on the sessions you’re interested in to secure your place. Spaces may be limited, so we encourage you to book early!
  • Confirm Your Booking: After selecting your sessions, please follow the prompts to complete your booking. You’ll receive a confirmation email with details about your chosen talks and tours.

Important Notes:

  • Tickets: Each visitor needs to book only one ticket for campus access, but additional tickets are required for individual talks and tours.
  • Check the App: Use the MySMU app to stay updated on any changes or announcements related to the schedule.

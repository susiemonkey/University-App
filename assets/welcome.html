Welcome to St Mary's Undergraduate Open Day!

We're delighted to have you here and thank you for joining us. Today is all about giving you the best insight into life at St Mary's and helping you discover everything our vibrant campus has to offer. Located in the heart of Twickenham, we're proud of our friendly and supportive community, and we hope you'll feel right at home as you explore.

To help you make the most of your visit, we encourage you to use the MySMU app, where you’ll find everything you need for a smooth and enjoyable day.

Here’s what the app can help you with:

  • Before the Open Day: Find a helpful checklist and tips to get ready for your visit.
  • How to get to St Mary’s: Directions and travel options to ensure you arrive easily.
  • Campus Maps: Navigate your way around our beautiful campus with ease.
  • Book tickets for talks & tours: Reserve your spot for subject talks, campus tours, and accommodation visits.
  • Accommodation: Explore our range of housing options to get a feel for student living.
  • FAQs: Got questions? Find answers to common queries here.
  • Food and drink: Discover where to grab a bite or a coffee during your visit.
  • Twickenham Life: Learn about the local area and what makes it a great place to live and study.
  • Open Day feedback survey: We value your feedback—don’t forget to share your thoughts on the day!

If you need help at any point, feel free to ask our friendly staff and student ambassadors. We hope you have a fantastic time exploring all that St Mary’s University has to offer!

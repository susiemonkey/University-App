Accommodation Options at St Mary's University

At St Mary's University, we offer a range of accommodation options designed to suit different needs and budgets. Our friendly and supportive community ensures that you will feel at home while studying with us.

Types of Accommodation

  • Halls of Residence: Modern, fully furnished rooms located within easy reach of the campus. Enjoy a vibrant community atmosphere with social activities and support available.
  • Shared Houses: A great option for students looking for a more independent living experience. These houses come equipped with shared kitchens and communal spaces.
  • Private Rentals: Explore a variety of private accommodation options available in Twickenham. Whether you're looking for a flat or a house share, you’ll find plenty of choices nearby.

Support and Resources

Our accommodation team is here to help you find the perfect place to live during your studies. We provide:

  • Advice on choosing the right accommodation type
  • Guidance on tenancy agreements and housing regulations
  • Support for any issues that may arise during your stay

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to contact us or visit the accommodation office on campus.

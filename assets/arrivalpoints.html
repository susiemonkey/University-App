Arrival Points for St Mary's University Open Day

Check-in Location

St Mary’s University, Main Campus Reception

When to Check In?

Morning Ticket Holders: 9.30am – 1.00pm

Afternoon Ticket Holders: 1.00pm – 4.00pm

Important Information

Please ensure you have your QR code ready, which you’ll find in the reminder email sent a few days before the Open Day. The QR code will be scanned for entry when you arrive on campus.

Plan your arrival time based on your schedule and the locations of your talks, tours, and activities. You can use the MySMU app for digital campus maps to help you navigate, and you’ll also receive a printed map when you check in.

Our friendly student ambassadors will be around to assist you and guide you between locations throughout your visit.

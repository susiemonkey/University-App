Before You Arrive: Checklist

Before you arrive at St Mary’s University for the Open Day, make sure you’ve completed these important steps for a smooth and enjoyable experience:

  • Explore the MySMU app: Discover the exciting talks, tours, and activities happening throughout the day.
  • Book your place: Reserve your spot for talks, tours, and other sessions through the app to ensure you don’t miss out.
  • Prepare questions: Write down any questions you want answered during your visit.
  • Have your QR code ready: Keep your QR code on your phone for quick and easy check-in upon arrival.
  • Plan your journey: Plan your trip to St Mary’s in Twickenham, allowing extra time to reach campus for your first activity.
  • Book train tickets: Consider booking train tickets in advance, especially if traveling from outside the local area.
  • Arrange overnight accommodation: Book nearby accommodation if you plan to stay overnight (if applicable).
  • Special assistance: Contact us at opendays@stmarys.ac.uk if you need any special assistance on the day.

By ticking off this checklist, you’ll ensure everything is set for a great Open Day at St Mary’s University!
